# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance, and it's essential to understand the risks associated with damaged auto glass. A cracked or shattered windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle, putting you and your passengers at risk of injury.

## Identify Auto Glass Damage
To ensure your safety on the road, it's crucial to identify auto glass damage promptly. Here are some common signs to look out for:
* Chips or cracks in the windshield
* Discoloration or haziness in the glass
* Water leaks or condensation between the glass layers

## The Risks of Ignoring Auto Glass Damage
Ignoring auto glass damage can lead to severe consequences, including:
* Reduced visibility, increasing the risk of accidents
* Compromised vehicle structure, which can affect airbag deployment and passenger safety
* Increased risk of ejection in the event of an accident
