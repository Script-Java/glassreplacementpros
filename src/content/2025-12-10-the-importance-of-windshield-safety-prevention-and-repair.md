# Introduction to Windshield Safety 
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that ensures the well-being of drivers and passengers. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of a vehicle and increase the risk of injury or fatality in the event of an accident. 
## Why Windshield Safety Matters 
* A windshield provides up to 30% of a vehicle's structural strength 
* It helps to prevent roof collapse in the event of a rollover 
* A damaged windshield can obstruct the driver's view, leading to accidents 
## Prevention is Key 
Regular inspection and maintenance of a windshield can help to prevent damage and ensure safety on the road. This includes: 
* Checking for chips and cracks 
* Avoiding extreme temperature changes 
* Parking in shaded areas to reduce exposure to the sun
