# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that ensures the well-being of drivers and passengers. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle, putting you at risk of injury or even fatal accidents.
## Why Windshield Safety Matters
* A windshield provides up to 70% of the vehicle's structural integrity in a rollover accident
* It helps to prevent ejection of occupants from the vehicle in the event of a crash
* A damaged windshield can obstruct the driver's view, leading to decreased reaction time and increased risk of accidents
## Types of Windshield Damage
* **Chip**: A small, circular crack in the windshield, typically caused by a stone or other debris
* **Crack**: A longer, more extensive crack that can spread across the windshield
* **Shatter**: A complete breakage of the windshield, often caused by severe impact
## Repair or Replace: What to Do
If you notice any damage to your windshield, it's essential to take immediate action. For small chips and cracks, repair may be a viable option. However, if the damage is more extensive, replacement may be necessary.
## Conclusion
Windshield safety is not something to be taken lightly. By understanding the importance of windshield safety and taking prompt action when damage occurs, you can help ensure your safety on the road.
