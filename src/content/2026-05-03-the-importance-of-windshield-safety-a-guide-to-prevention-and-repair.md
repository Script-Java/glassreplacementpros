# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that is often overlooked until it's too late. A damaged windshield can pose a significant risk to drivers and passengers, compromising the structural integrity of the vehicle and increasing the likelihood of injury or accident.

## Why Windshield Safety Matters
* A windshield provides up to 60% of a vehicle's structural integrity in the event of a rollover accident
* A damaged windshield can obstruct the driver's view, leading to reduced reaction time and increased stopping distance
* Windshields are designed to work in conjunction with airbags, and a damaged windshield can compromise the effectiveness of these safety devices

## Prevention is Key
To avoid the risks associated with a damaged windshield, it's essential to take preventative measures:
* Regularly inspect your windshield for chips and cracks
* Avoid driving over potholes or debris that can cause damage
* Use a windshield treatment to improve visibility during rain or snow
